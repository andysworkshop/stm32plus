Detailed Description

Free cluster finder suitable for flash media.

Find a free cluster by randomly picking a position in the FAT and then iterating until a free entry is found. Scattering writes is desirable for flash devices that will wear out after a certain number of writes. Since the FAT blocks are heavily hit during write activity the benefit of this approach is limited unless the FAT is cached.
